Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at Happy Hours. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our services. By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your data in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information when you interact with our website and services:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Event preferences and booking details
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party services)

This information is necessary to provide you with event booking services, facilitate communication, and ensure smooth event execution.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Processing event bookings and managing reservations
  • Sending event updates, confirmations, and reminders
  • Providing customer support and resolving issues related to bookings
  • Improving our website and services based on your feedback
  • Sending marketing communications (only if you have opted in to receive them)

We do not use your personal information for any other purposes without your consent.

3. Data Security

Your personal information is stored securely in compliance with industry standards. We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure, and we cannot ensure absolute security of your data.

4. Third-Party Services

To provide our services, we may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers. These may include:

  • Payment processors to securely handle transactions
  • Event logistics partners for coordinating event details
  • Email service providers for sending event-related updates and promotional materials

These third parties are required to adhere to the same privacy standards as outlined in this policy and are prohibited from using your information for any other purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website, analyze website traffic, and improve our services. Cookies help us remember your preferences and track your interaction with our website.

You can control your cookie settings through your browser. Please note that disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Update: You can request corrections to any inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal obligations.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our contact page, and we will respond to your request in a timely manner.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.
